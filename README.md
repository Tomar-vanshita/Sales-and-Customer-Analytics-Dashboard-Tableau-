# Sales and Customer Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales and customer data using Tableau to understand revenue performance, customer trends, and business growth opportunities.

The dashboards are designed to provide a clear overview of key metrics while allowing users to explore detailed insights related to sales performance and customer behavior.

---

## Why This Project?
I created this project to strengthen my ability to design business-focused dashboards that combine both sales performance and customer analytics.

The objective was to build clear and interactive visualizations that stakeholders can use to monitor growth, identify trends, and make informed decisions.

---

## Business Objectives
- Track overall sales and revenue trends
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify high-value customers and segments
- Monitor sales performance by region or category
- Support data-driven sales and marketing strategies

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Tableau**
  - Interactive dashboards
  - Filters and drill-down analysis
  - KPI visualizations
- **Excel / CSV** (data source)

## Dataset Information
- **Source:** Company-like sales dataset
- **File:** `Sales & Customer Dashboards.twbx`
- **Data Includes:**
  - Order ID
  - Order Date
  - Customer Name / ID
  - Product Category
  - Sales Amount
  - Quantity
  - Region
  - Profit (if applicable)
